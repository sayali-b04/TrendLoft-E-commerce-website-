An item is missing from my order.

Our shipments go through rigorous quality check processes before they leave our warehouse. However, in the rare case of a missing item in your order, you can request for a replacement or refund within 7 days of receiving the order: Step 1: Follow one of the following steps: a. Via My Orders section Click on 'My Orders'. Go to Order and select 'Return' or 'Replace'. Proceed with reason for return. b. Via Help Center Click on 'Have a Concern'>Click on 'Submit a Request' > Provide us with your order ID details and your request to return/replace your order. Elaborate your concern about missing product Step 2: Once your request is received, We will provide you resolution within 72 hours. NOTE: If it is a case of replacement, it is subject to the availability of stock. In cases when a replacement may not be available, we will refund you the full amount.
