OWENERS GUIDE

We want you to love what you ordered, but if something is not right, let us know. You may return the merchandise and place a new order for another colour, or a different size, or an entirely new style.

In the unlikely event that any merchandise you have ordered from us is not received in good condition, damaged or defective, or if merchandise delivered, is different from what you had ordered, you may return the merchandise. For the process of return, the product should be unused and in the same condition as you received it with its original packaging & the tags. The merchandise return is valid within 7 days from the date on which these goods were delivered.

We do not return or exchange gift certificates, jewellery, fragrances and sunglasses once dispatched, unless it is a manufacturing defect.

Please Note:

It is to be explicitly noted that in case of return requests, you shall be eligible for returns only up to 40% of the total orders successfully placed and processed by you w.r.t your account with Ochre and Black Private Limited. In the event of your returns reaching/crossing 40% or more, our system shall automatically generate a warning email to you on your email address registered with us which has been linked to your account.

In cases where the returns shall reach/exceed 50% of the total orders successfully placed and processed by you, our system shall automatically block the “returns” tab on your account and no further return requests shall be processed until the return quantum is back within the aforementioned trigger limit.
